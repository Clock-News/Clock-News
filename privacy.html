Privacy Policy

At The Undertime, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Who We Are

Undertime is an independently run website managed by a single individual based in Pakistan. The site publishes informational content on world affairs, science, history, and sports.

2. What Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via a contact form). However, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information through cookies and analytics tools, including:

  • Device type and browser
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring websites
  • General location data (e.g., country)

3. Use of Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve content, layout, and performance.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Device and browser type
  • Time spent on pages
  • Click behavior and navigation flow

Google Analytics does not collect personal data like your name, email, or contact details.

To learn more, see Google’s policies

4. Cookies and Consent

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly (necessary cookies)
  • Analyze site traffic (analytical cookies via Google Analytics)

We manage cookie preferences using the Silktide Cookie Consent Banner, which gives you control over which cookies you accept.

Cookie Categories:

Type Description
Necessary Essential for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
Analytical Help us understand user behavior to improve the site. Optional.

5. How We Store and Protect Data

We do not store any personally identifiable information on our servers. Non-personal data collected via cookies is processed and stored securely by Google under their privacy practices.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party data processors we use are:

  • Google Analytics (analytics)
  • Silktide (cookie consent)

Both comply with international privacy standards (GDPR, etc.).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we collect about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw cookie consent at any time

To make a request, contact us via the Contact page.

8. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy and data protection laws in Pakistan and globally, including:

  • Electronic Transactions Ordinance, 2002
  • Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA), 2016
  • Best practices under GDPR and Google’s consent guidelines

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the site implies acceptance of any revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out via the Contact page.
