What You Should Know

Clock News is a digital news platform that collects news from publicly available and credible international and regional sources. We present it in a clean, simple format — often adding summaries, background, or editorial notes to help make things clearer. We work independently under Pakistani law and have no ties to any of the news organizations we include.

Our Approach

  • News Sources: We rely on publicly accessible stories from respected outlets such as BBC, Reuters, Al Jazeera, and Dawn, among others. Full credit is always given.
  • No Original Reporting: We don’t conduct our own journalism or field reporting. All content is based on already-published information.
  • Explanations & Summaries: While we don’t change the meaning of the news we share, we often explain it in simpler terms, break it down, or add helpful context. These summaries or headlines are written by our editorial team.
  • No Claim of Ownership: We don’t own the original news content. Copyright for those articles belongs to the original publishers. However, we do own the layout, formatting, summaries, and editorial voice of our website.
  • Fair Use: All content is shared under fair use principles — for public awareness, education, and commentary. We don’t republish full articles or distribute news content for commercial gain.
  • No Official Ties: Clock News is not affiliated with or endorsed by any news outlet mentioned on this site. All trademarks remain the property of their respective owners.
  • Presentation Style: We present curated news in our unique layout and design — making the content easier to explore and understand, without altering the facts.
  • Accuracy & Liability: We aim for accuracy, but we rely on external reporting. We don’t guarantee that every detail is always up-to-date or error-free. Readers should verify important information directly from the source.
  • External Links: Some content may link to third-party websites. We’re not responsible for their content or privacy practices.
  • Sponsored Content: We may display ads or sponsored posts. These will always be marked. Sponsorship does not affect how we choose or explain the news.
  • Changes to This Disclaimer: This page may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website means you accept any updates.
