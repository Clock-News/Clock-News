About us

Subworldnews is an independent digital news platform focused on delivering timely, relevant, and clear news from South Asia and the wider world. Run by a dedicated individual, the platform aims to make important stories accessible to everyone without bias or sensationalism.

We focus on providing brief, straightforward coverage of key events, politics, sports, technology, and more. Our goal is to give voice to local perspectives while connecting them to a global audience. SubWorldnews believes in honest reporting and content that respects the reader's time and intelligence.
